Hometown Supermarket is an international supermarket situated at 808 Green Springs Highway #140, Homewood, AL 35209. With over 40,000 square feet, our market offers over 100 thousand different products catering to the needs of our customers belonging to different ethnic backgrounds.

Our Products

Some major product categories offered in our store include fresh vegetables, fruits, seafood, meat, along with canned goods, frozen food, cleaning products, plants, houseware, and cookware. In addition, we also carry tobacco and alcohol products, Chinese herbal medicine, and Korean beauty supplies. While many of our products come from China, Vietnam, Thailand, Korea, Japan, the Philippines, and Indonesia, we also offer food and cooking products from India, the Middle East, Mexico, Central America, Africa, Jamaica, other Caribbean islands, and the United States.
